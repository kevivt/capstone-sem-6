## 4) Threshold Methodology and Derived Values

Thresholds are generated by `build_risk_profiles.py` (not manually hardcoded).

Method:

1. Train disease model on `*_train_smote.csv`.
2. Score held-out `*_test.csv` using probability-like outputs.
3. Compute best moderate threshold by maximizing F1 over candidate score cutoffs.
4. Stabilize deployment bands:

- moderate clipped to [0.35, 0.75]
- high = max(85th percentile score, moderate + 0.15), capped at 0.90
- enforce high > moderate with minimum gap when needed

Risk bands:

- low: score < moderate
- moderate: moderate <= score < high
- high: score >= high

### Current deployed thresholds

- Chronic Kidney Disease: moderate 0.75, high 0.90
- Hypertension: moderate 0.488726, high 0.90
- Type Two Diabetes Mellitus: moderate 0.389735, high 0.661201

## 5) Hyperparameters and Factor Interpretation

### Chronic Kidney Disease (K-Nearest Neighbors Classifier)

- `n_neighbors=1`
- `weights='distance'`
- `algorithm='kd_tree'`

Interpretation:

- Very local decision boundary, high sensitivity to nearest clinical pattern.
- Strong baseline performance but discrete score behavior, requiring threshold stabilization.

Top factors (current profile):

- Diabetes Mellitus History (dm), Hypertension History (htn), Urine Specific Gravity (sg), Appetite Status (appet), Pedal Edema (pe)

### Hypertension (Support Vector Classifier)

- `C=4`
- `kernel='rbf'`
- `probability=True`
- `random_state=42`

Interpretation:

- Nonlinear boundary captures blood-pressure, age, and medication interactions.
- Stable precision/recall balance for current preprocessed feature space.

Top factors (current profile):

- Systolic Blood Pressure (sysBP), Diastolic Blood Pressure (diaBP), Age (age), Blood Pressure Medication Use (BPMeds), Heart Rate (heartRate)

### Type Two Diabetes Mellitus (Random Forest Classifier)

- `n_estimators=120`
- `max_depth=4`
- `min_samples_leaf=125`
- `random_state=42`
- `n_jobs=-1`

Interpretation:

- Conservative forest tuned for robustness/generalization.
- Better recall orientation; precision is comparatively weaker in baseline.

Top factors (current profile):

- Plasma Glucose Concentration (Glucose), Two Hour Serum Insulin (Insulin), Body Mass Index (BMI), Triceps Skin Fold Thickness (SkinThickness), Age (Age)
